# Code Of Conduct

This project follows the NVIDIA open-source community expectation that contributors communicate respectfully, collaborate constructively, and keep technical discussions focused on improving the project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional.
- Assume good intent while asking clarifying questions.
- Keep feedback specific, actionable, and relevant to the work.
- Respect privacy and avoid sharing confidential information.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated disruption of issues, pull requests, or maintainer discussions.
- Sharing secrets, credentials, proprietary material, or security vulnerabilities in public channels.

## Reporting

For security vulnerabilities, follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). For conduct concerns in project spaces, contact the maintainers listed in [MAINTAINERS.md](MAINTAINERS.md).
